Create your first identity on the Evolution chain

An identity is your account on Dash Platform. You can't just "sign up" — it has to be funded with credits first. This walks you through every step and hands you a ready .env for the evo-cookbook at the end.

  • Generate a testnet wallet (offline, in your browser)
  • Fund its address via the Dash Bridge
  • Mint an identity from it
  • Claim a .dash username
  • Walk away with keys + a working .env
Testnet only. The wallet created here is for experimenting — never reuse this mnemonic on mainnet or with real funds.
